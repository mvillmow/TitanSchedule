The Friction of Fragmentation

Current tournament scheduling systems (like SportsEngine AES) scatter data across multiple pages. Determining a team's next location requires mentally calculating pool results and cross-referencing bracket logic, leading to confusion and delays.

Project Goal

Create a Single Monolithic Graph where the initial sort (seeds) is on the left, and the final sort (standings) is on the right. Updates happen in real-time as games finish.
